What We Self-Perform

Full-Scope Fencing & Perimeter Capabilities

From the first survey stake to final punch list, our crews self-perform the work — no layered subs, no schedule risk.

High-Security Perimeter

K-rated and ASTM F2656 anti-ram fence lines, 8'–12' security enclosures, and intrusion-resistant barriers for sensitive sites.

Chain Link & Welded Wire

Galvanized and vinyl-coated chain link, welded mesh, and PVC systems engineered for industrial and utility spec compliance.

Ornamental Steel & Aluminum

Architectural picket and palisade fencing with industrial coatings — secure perimeters that still meet design review.

Anti-Ram & Crash Barriers

Crash-rated gates, bollards, wedge barriers, and cable-reinforced fence for force-protection and DoD UFC standards.

Automated Access Control

Slide and swing operators, card/biometric readers, loop detection, and gate integration with site security networks.

Detention & Correctional

Maximum-security enclosures, razor coil, anti-climb mesh, and sally ports built to correctional facility specifications.

Substation & Critical Infrastructure

NERC CIP-compliant utility enclosures, grounding-tied fence systems, and screening for energy and water assets.

Sports, Aviation & Recreation

Airport perimeter (FAA), ballfield backstops, tennis enclosures, and high-tension systems for large public facilities.

Temporary & Construction

Rapid-deploy panel fencing, jobsite security enclosures, and phased temporary perimeters for active project sites.
